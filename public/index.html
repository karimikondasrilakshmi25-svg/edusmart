Frequently Asked Questions
How does the AI video explanation work?›
EduSmart uses Groq AI to generate clear, curriculum-level explanations for any topic. The explanation is then read aloud using your device's voice engine, with words highlighted as they are spoken.
How does the AI chatbot work?›
The AI chatbot is powered by Groq AI (free!). It can answer hard academic questions, solve maths step-by-step, explain scientific concepts, and much more. Tap the 🤖 button to open it anytime.
Which languages are supported?›
Voice playback supports English, Telugu, Hindi, Tamil, Kannada, and Malayalam. Go to Profile → Voice Language to change it.
